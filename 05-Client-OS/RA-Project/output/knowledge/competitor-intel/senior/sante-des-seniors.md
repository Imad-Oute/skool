## Raw Notes

- Uses "courtier en mutuelles seniors depuis 1991" — the 35-year history is the primary trust anchor
- The "garanti le moins cher" is a bold price promise — no proof mechanism shown on landing page
- 20% discount promotion extension appears — price-led aggressive acquisition
- NO urgency triggers (Châtel window = November–January is completely unexploited)
- NO named advisor — purely transactional relationship
- Mondial Assistance partnership adds credibility but is a generic insurance add-on, not differentiating
- Leads go into a form funnel, then presumably called back — slower conversion path than direct call
