## Raw Notes

- **CRITICAL FINDING:** zen-senior.fr and sante-des-seniors.fr use THE EXACT SAME headline on their landing pages ("LA COMPLÉMENTAIRE SANTÉ DES SÉNIORS"), the same "depuis 1991" claim, the same Mondial Assistance partnership, the same form structure, and nearly identical ad copy. These two are almost certainly the same operator running two domains to dominate more ad inventory — or one is a direct clone of the other.
- zen-senior.fr has dramatically higher impression volume (250-300k) suggesting larger ad budget
- The €50 referral bonus is unique — they're leveraging senior community word-of-mouth
- The 20% discount price extension is aggressive — signals they're in a price war
- NO exploitation of seasonal triggers (Châtel window, dental cost season)
- NO named human face — purely a price aggregator positioning
- Their dominant angle (price) is their vulnerability: the senior avatar FEARS "low price = low quality/service" — Rolland can turn their strength into a doubt trigger
