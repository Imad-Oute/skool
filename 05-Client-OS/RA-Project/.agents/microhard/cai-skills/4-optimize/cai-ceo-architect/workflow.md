# CEO Architect Workflow

## Purpose

Strategic oversight for Client Acquisition Intelligence campaigns. This workflow validates business models, sets strategic direction, and ensures executive-level decision quality for acquisition strategies.

## Role Definition

**The CEO Architect** provides the 30,000-foot view. While other agents execute tactics (content, hooks, ads), the CEO validates:

- Is this the right strategy for the target market?
- Do the unit economics work (LTGP:CAC)?
- Are we building a sustainable acquisition engine?
- What are the strategic risks and opportunities?

## Step Sequence

This workflow executes in 6 sequential steps:

1. **step-01-init.md** - Initialize strategic planning session
2. **step-02-business-model-validation.md** - Validate unit economics and business model
3. **step-03-strategic-direction.md** - Set strategic priorities and direction
4. **step-04-risk-assessment.md** - Identify strategic risks and mitigation
5. **step-05-resource-allocation.md** - Plan resource allocation and scaling
6. **step-06-finalize.md** - Finalize strategic plan and next steps

## Output Structure

The workflow produces a strategic plan document containing:

- Business model validation (LTGP:CAC, profitability gates)
- Strategic priorities and direction
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Resource allocation plan
- Success metrics and KPIs

## Integration with Other CAI Skills

- **Before Content/Ads:** CEO validates strategy is sound
- **After Initial Results:** CEO reviews performance and adjusts strategy
- **Quarterly:** CEO runs strategic review using this workflow

## Menu Navigation

Each step provides menu options to navigate forward, backward, or jump to specific steps as needed.
