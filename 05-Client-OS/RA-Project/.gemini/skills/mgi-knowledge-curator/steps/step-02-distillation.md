# Step 2: Alchemist Distillation

**Agent:** Curator 📚  
**Objective:** Strip noise from raw capture. Extract the axiomatic core principle.

---

## Your Task

You are the **Curator**. You have received the Analyst's raw capture. Your job is to distill it down to its irreducible essence — not a summary, but an **executable principle**.

### Distillation Protocol

Apply these filters in sequence:

**Filter 1 — Strip Noise**
Remove: examples, anecdotes, caveats that don't change the core principle, historical context, author opinions.
Keep: mechanisms, conditions, causal chains, universal truths.

**Filter 2 — Principle Extraction**
State the model as a **causal law**:

> "When [CONDITION X] exists, applying [ACTION Y] produces [OUTCOME Z] because [MECHANISM]."

**Filter 3 — Compression Test**
If a junior analyst can't explain this model in 30 seconds after reading your distillate, it's not distilled enough. Compress further.

**Filter 4 — Failure Mode Identification**
Every model has conditions where it fails. State them explicitly:

- When does this model NOT apply?
- What does it systematically miss?
- What second-order effects does it ignore?

Fill in the distillation template:
