# Here are the 6 dashboards I’ve seen transform businesses:  
  
1️⃣ Executive Dashboard  
→ The “bird’s-eye view.” Revenue, CAC, churn, pipeline.  
→ If leadership checks only one thing daily, it’s this.  
  
2️⃣ Operational Dashboard  
→Real-time updates on what's breaking, what’s delayed, and what needs fixing NOW.  
  
3️⃣ Financial Dashboard  
→ Cashflow, burn rate, runway, expenses.  
→ It tells you whether you’re building a business… or burning one.  
  
4️⃣ Marketing Performance Dashboard  
→ Campaign ROI, impressions, CPL, conversions.  
→ No more guessing what’s working.  
  
5️⃣ Sales Dashboard  
→ Targets, win rates, deal velocity, top reps.  
→ A mirror that shows exactly where revenue leaks.  
  
6️⃣ Customer Success Dashboard  
→ NPS, ticket trends, retention, upsell opportunities.  
→ Because the real growth engine is happy customers.
