# Ecosystem Blueprint: Chapter 4 - Run Paid Ads (Part II)

## 1. Macro Level: The Efficiency Foundation
The objective of this layer is to ensure fiscal viability by optimizing the relationship between acquisition costs and total returns.

| Component | Definition | Validation Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Scaling Phases** | Track -> Lose (Invest) -> Print (Scale). | Cashflow velocity (Time to break-even). |
| **Efficiency Benchmarks** | Comparing LTGP (Lifetime Gross Profit) to CAC (Cost to Acquire). | LTGP : CAC Ratio (Target > 3:1). |
| **Client Financed Acquisition** | Covering acquisition + fulfillment costs within 30 days. | Cash-on-hand recovery window (Target <= 30 days). |

---

## 2. Meso Level: Money Stuff (The Math)
The tactical financial controls to manage ad spend and profitability.

| Component | Definition | Validation Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Test Budget** | 2x the 30-day cash collected per customer. | Spend threshold vs. Lead generation rate. |
| **Kill Switch** | Stopping spend if performance targets aren't met. | Lead acquisition cost vs. 30-day cash-collected. |
| **Upsell Engine** | Increasing profit per user to subsidize CAC. | Average Order Value (AOV) increase per customer. |

---

## 3. Micro Level: Optimization & Strategy
Operational systems to expand reach safely.

| Component | Definition | Validation Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Scaling Logic** | Reversing budget based on sales capacity + 20% pad. | Budget utilization rate vs. Capacity. |
| **Sales vs. Ads** | Diagnosing the problem (is it the ad or the sales process?). | Conversion rate from lead to customer. |
| **Content Recycling** | Using high-performing free content/UGC as ads. | Ad performance vs. original content performance. |

---

## 4. Operational Feedback Loop
*   **Rule:** "Ads are a money-printing machine."
*   **System Check:** If an ad isn't profitable:
    *   **Is it the CAC?** (Bad offer/targeting/creative).
    *   **Is it the LTGP?** (Bad business model/upsells).
*   **Action:** If your CAC is above 3x industry average, fix the ad (CAC). If CAC is below 3x, fix the business model (LTGP).
*   **Growth:** When profitable, spend as much as operations can handle.
