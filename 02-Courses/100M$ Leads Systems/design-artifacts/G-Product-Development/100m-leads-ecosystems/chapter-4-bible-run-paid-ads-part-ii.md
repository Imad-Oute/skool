# Bible of Chapter 4: Run Paid Ads (Part II) - Money Stuff

## 1. Operating Rules (The Financial Non-Negotiables)
*   **The Golden Ratio (LTGP:CAC):** Target >3:1. If <3:1, you are bleeding equity.
*   **The 30-Day Rule:** If you can't cover CAC + fulfillment within 30 days, your cash flow will kill your scale.
*   **The Investment Mindset:** Ads are not an expense; they are an investment in a "money printer." You *will* lose money testing. That is the price of the machine.
*   **The "Puddle to Ocean" Scale:** Don't bet the farm. Prove profitability in a small segment, then expand.

---

## 2. The Scaling System (Phases)
1.  **Track Money:** Before spending $1, implement tracking. Without it, you are gambling, not investing.
2.  **Invest (Lose) Money:** Budget 2x the 30-day cash value of a customer to test.
    *   *No leads?* Kill at 1x cost.
    *   *Leads but no profit?* Kill at 2x cost.
    *   *Winner?* Scale 10x immediately.
3.  **Print Money:** If it works, spend as much as your sales/fulfillment capacity allows (plus a 20% pad).

---

## 3. Financial Diagnostics & Logic
*   **LTGP vs. LTV:** Ignore standard LTV. Calculate *Lifetime Gross Profit* (Price - Delivery Cost). This is the only number that pays rent/payroll.
*   **Sales vs. Ads Problem:** If leads are coming in but not buying, *do not change the ads*. Fix the sales process. The ads are doing their job.
*   **The Upsell Fix:** If the first purchase is unprofitable, add an upsell (100% margin). If you can make $20-30 in extra profit in 30 days, you turn "expensive leads" into "profitable leads."

---

## 4. Knowledge Bank: Key Takeaways
*   **Business Model > Ads:** Lowering CAC has a ceiling; increasing LTGP has no ceiling. If your CAC is already at industry average, *do not* obsess over more efficiency—obsess over your business model.
*   **Consistency:** The "Submit" button isn't just for ads; it's the mindset for *all* growth.
*   **Patience:** Ads are slow at first. Don't stop too early.

---

## 5. Paid Ad Execution Checklist
- [ ] **Track Setup:** Pixel/Google Analytics configured correctly.
- [ ] **Capacity Check:** Do I have the sales capacity for 100+ new leads/mo?
- [ ] **Budget Set:** Calculated 2x CAC for testing.
- [ ] **The "Jugular" Ask:** Ensure the landing page offer is aligned with the ad creative.
- [ ] **The Upsell:** Is there a 100% margin upsell ready for new buyers?
- [ ] **Testing:** Am I changing one variable at a time?
